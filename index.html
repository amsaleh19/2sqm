Our Story

Furnishing a home can feel overwhelming. 2 Sqm was created to make interior design easier—especially for small apartments and compact rooms.

We focus on practical layouts, modern aesthetics, and comfort. Whether you’re refreshing one room or planning a full makeover, we help you choose the right pieces and design direction.
